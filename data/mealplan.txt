### Meal Plan

Here's a healthy dinner meal plan for a week with whole foods and one cheat day included.
Remember to adjust portion sizes and ingredients based on your dietary preferences and caloric needs.

### Outline Summary
1. Day 1: Grilled Chicken Salad
2. Day 2: Baked Salmon
3. Day 3: Vegetarian Stir-Fry
4. Day 4: Mediterranean Chickpea Bowl
5. Day 5: Beef and Broccoli
6. Day 6: Roasted Vegetable Pasta7
7. Day 7 (Cheat Day): Your Favorite Indulgence
8. Day 8: Grilled Shrimp Tacos

### Full Plan

**Day 1: Grilled Chicken Salad**
- Grilled chicken breast with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a balsamic vinaigrette dressing.
- Side of quinoa or brown rice.

**Day 2: Baked Salmon**
- Baked salmon with a lemon-dill sauce.
- Steamed broccoli and a side of sweet potato.

**Day 3: Vegetarian Stir-Fry**
- Tofu or tempeh stir-fried with bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, and a low-sodium teriyaki sauce.
- Serve over brown rice or cauliflower rice.

**Day 4: Mediterranean Chickpea Bowl**
- Chickpeas roasted with olive oil, garlic, and spices.
- Serve over a bed of spinach, with diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and feta cheese. Drizzle with tahini dressing.

**Day 5: Beef and Broccoli**
- Lean beef stir-fried with broccoli and a homemade sauce (low-sodium soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and honey).
- Serve with quinoa or whole wheat noodles.

**Day 6: Roasted Vegetable Pasta**
- Whole wheat pasta with roasted cherry tomatoes, zucchini, bell peppers, and red onion.
- Toss with a light olive oil and herb sauce, and top with grated Parmesan cheese.

**Day 7 (Cheat Day): Your Favorite Indulgence**
- Enjoy your favorite meal, whether it's pizza, burgers, or something sweet.
- Remember to practice portion control and enjoy it guilt-free!

**Day 8: Grilled Shrimp Tacos**
- Grilled shrimp with taco seasoning, served in whole wheat tortillas.
- Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado slices, and a yogurt-based cilantro-lime sauce.
